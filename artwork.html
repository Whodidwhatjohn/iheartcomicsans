Background

One could say that I am a relatively recent addition to the Human race, sitting at the grand old age of 22, but in that short time frame, I have developed a deep appreciation for art. Art of all kinds, shapes, and sizes. Paintings, Pottery, Poetry, Storytelling; I love it all.

Many times over the years I have attempted to teach myself to draw. I can never stick with it for very long though, thanks to a bad bout of ADHD. I have, however, stuck to it long enough to become decent-ish at it, but in the meantime, I have also taught myself numerous other skills in the artistic realm. I have turned my hand at 3D modelling, I have tried poetry, I have also dabbled in cooking. All of those things, I would consider Art.

Down below, you will find links to several galleries, curated by myself, featuring numerous pieces of artwork, photography, 3D models, or otherwise, which I have created by my own hand. There will also be galleries of images that I find interesting, but which I did not create myself. Permission will be gathered where needed, and credit given to the original artists.
